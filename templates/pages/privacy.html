Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ now|date:"F d, Y" }}

1. Who We Are

Future Skill (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a learning platform providing live and offline instructor-led courses. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, phone, profile info, role (student/instructor/admin).
  • Course data: enrollments, attendance, lesson completion, certificates.
  • Payment data: transaction IDs, provider, amount, status (we do not store full card/bank details).
  • Device/usage data: IP, browser, logs (for security and analytics).

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve courses, track progress, issue certificates.
  • Process payments and verify transactions.
  • Secure accounts, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Send service emails (enrollment, schedule, payment status). Marketing emails only with consent.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Limited sharing with:

  • Instructors (for enrolled students’ names, progress, attendance).
  • Payment processors (transaction verification).
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics) under confidentiality.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain account, enrollment, and certificate records as long as needed for service and legal purposes. Payment records are kept per compliance requirements.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your profile (subject to legal/record-keeping needs).
  • Opt-out of marketing emails.
  • Request a copy of your data or raise concerns via support.

7. Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls, and monitoring. No system is 100% secure; contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.

8. Cookies

We use essential and analytics cookies to keep you logged in, maintain sessions, and improve the platform. See our Cookie Policy for details.

9. Children

Future Skill is for users 16+ (or local age of consent). Underage users require guardian consent where applicable.
